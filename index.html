About

Alexandra L. Ramos Figueroa

I'm currently a Chemical Engineering Ph.D. Candidate at Stanford University under the advisement of Prof. Zhenan Bao. My research focuses on engineering self-healing multilayer platforms for soft electronics, with a focus on how molecular architecture governs interfacial behavior, selective healing, and autonomous alignment in damaged multilayer systems.

My work combines supramolecular chemistry, interfacial engineering, and nanoscale characterization to design dynamic soft materials capable of maintaining function following different degrees of damage. I am particularly interested in translating this expertise to design new materials tailored for women's healthcare applications.

Outside the lab, I'm passionate about science communication and mentoring students from underrepresented backgrounds in STEM.
